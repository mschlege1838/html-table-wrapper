Manhattan (Traditional) Maker's Mark
  • Bourbon(2 parts)
  • Sweet Vermouth(1 part)
  • Bitters(2 dashes)
  • Maraschino Cherry
  1. Combine Bourbon, Vermouth and Bitters in a mixing glass
  2. Stir and strain into a chilled cocktail glass neat, or on the rocks
  3. Garnish with cherry
Manhattan (Traditional) Allrecipes
  • Rye(2 oz)
  • Sweet Vermouth(1/2 oz)
  • Bitters(1 dash)
  • Maraschino Cherry
  1. Combine rye, vermouth, and bitters in a cocktail mixing class. Add ice and stir until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Manhattan (Perfect) Liquor.com
  • Rye(3/4 oz)
  • Dry Vermouth(1/4 oz)
  • Sweet Vermouth(1/2 oz)
  • Orange Bitters(1 dash)
  1. Add the rye and both vermouths to a mixing glass and fill with ice.
  2. Stir, and strain into a rocks glass filled with fresh ice.
  3. Top with the bitters and garnish with a Maraschino cherry.
Old Fashioned Maker's Mark
  • Bourbon(1-1/2 parts)
  • Water(1 splash)
  • Bitters(2 dashes)
  • Sugar(1 tsp)
  • Orange(1 peel)
  • Maraschino Cherry
  1. Dissolve or muddle sugar with a splash of water in an Old Fashioned glass.
  2. Fill glass 3/4 full of ice.
  3. Add bourbon and bitters.
  4. Garnish with orange peel and cherry.
Old Fashioned (Muddled) Maker's Mark
  • Bourbon(1-1/2 parts)
  • Water(1 splash)
  • Bitters(2 dashes)
  • Sugar(1 tsp)
  • Orange(1 slice)
  • Maraschino Cherry
  1. Muddle sugar, bitters, orange slice, cherry and water in an Old Fashioned glass.
  2. Remove the orange rind.
  3. Fill glass 3/4 full of ice.
  4. Add borbon and stir.
Old Fashioned Allrecipes
  • Simple Syrup(2 tsp)
  • Water(1 tsp)
  • Bitters(2 dashes)
  • Ice Cubes(1 cup)
  • Bourbon(1-1/2 oz)
  • Orange(1 slice)
  • Maraschino Cherry
  1. Pour the simple syrup, water, and bitters into a whiskey glass.
  2. Stir to combine, then place the ice cubes in the glass.
  3. Pour bourbon over the ice and garnish with the orange slice and maraschino cherry.
Old Fashioned Esquire
  • Bourbon(2 oz)
  • Bitters(2 dashes)
  • Sugar(1 cube)
  • Club Soda
  • Orange(1 twist)
  1. Place the sugar cube in an Old Fashioned glass.
  2. Wet it down with Angostura bitters and a short splash of club soda.
  3. Crush the sugar with a wooden muddler, then rotate the glass so that the sugar grains and bitters give it a lining.
  4. Add a large ice cube. Pour in the bourbon.
  5. Garnish with an orange twist, and serve with a stirring rod if you're so inclined.
Cosmopolitan Allrecipes
  • Vodka(1-1/2 oz)
  • Lime Juice(1/4 oz)
  • Triple Sec(1/4 oz)
  • Cranberry Juice(1/4 oz)
  • Ice Cubes(1 cup)
  • Lime(1 wedge)
  1. Combine vodka, lime juice, triple sec, and cranberry juice in a cocktail shaker. Add ice, cover and shake until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  2. Garnish with a lime wedge.
